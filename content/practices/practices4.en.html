Important changes in the Ukrainian and international employment law and practice have a big impact on global and domestic businesses. In the process of expanding globalization of the economy the labour law is gaining growing importance. Both big corporations and small companies in Ukraine need the professional legal advice on labour law and related issues.

Over the years, we have built a solid labour law practice representing our clients in various matters, including top-end labour-related projects and labour litigation. Based on our long-term practical experience we provide advice on a multitude of labour law issues, including preparation and negotiation of labour collective agreements, drafting employment contracts for top management and employment agreements for all members of stuff, legal support in dismissal of personnel, as well as court disputes concerning labour matters.

Our lawyers have extensive experience advising corporate management on all aspects of labour and employment-related matters. We take a preventive approach by stressing our clients' rights and obligations early on to avoid potential problems, and in the case of employee disputes, we try to develop fast and cost-effective solutions that reflect our clients' objectives and interests. Related projects:

  • Advising Ukrainian enterprises and foreign representative offices on optimization of work stuff reduction of work stuff and massive corporate layoffs
  • Advising foreign business on structuring of compensation plans with key employees
  • Advising on employment and related disputes.
