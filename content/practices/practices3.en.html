Our attorneys regularly and intensively advise an increasing number of foreign and domestic clients on a wide spectrum of corporate matters including the structuring of operations and activities in Ukraine, establishing of new legal entities; corporate reorganization; liquidation of legal entities; drafting foundation documents, by-laws and other corporate documents; conducting general meetings of shareholders/participants; corporate governance; corporate restructuring; protecting shareholders rights; conducting legal due diligence, etc.

We offer overall legal and practical approach to handling the challenging corporate restructurings, reorganizations and setting up the optimal legal and tax structure for your businesses.

We provide services in the following areas:

  • Advising on corporate, structuring and restructuring issues
  • Advising on various issues of cross-border and domestic mergers and acquisitions, including antitrust and tax planning, due diligence and securities law advice
  • Formation of Companies - establishing limited liability and joint stock companies, banks, various types of investment funds and financial institutions, insurance companies, securities brokerage companies, charities, as well as branches and representative offices, including for foreign banks
  • Assistance with privatization and divestiture matters
  • Corporate Governance - advising potential and proposed investors, shareholders, corporate boards and management on various issues of corporate governance, including development of efficient governance structures and internal controls, asset and other controls, drafting charters, shareholder agreements, internal regulations and policies, consulting on executive compensation issues, developing employee stock option and compensation plans
  • Joint ventures - advising on formation and operation of joint ventures, including for oil and gas exploration, mineral resources, development and production.

Related projects:

  • Advising a European Bank on the successful acquisition of a Ukrainian bank
