We provide a full range of litigation services. We represent our clients before courts of general jurisdiction and commercial courts of first instance, courts of appeal, the Supreme Commercial Court of Ukraine and the Supreme Court of Ukraine.

We assist our clients on complex supply chain, customer/consumer and related disputes throughout the world. We assist our clients in matters regarding execution in Ukraine of foreign judgments and arbitral awards. We also have experience in practice devoted to representing clients in administrative litigation procedures before a wide range of governmental bodies, including the State Tax Administration of Ukraine, the State Customs Service of Ukraine, the Antimonopoly Committee of Ukraine, the Ministry of Economy of Ukraine, the State Committee on Standardization and Consumer Policy, the National Bank of Ukraine.

Our attorneys have extensive experience in the conduct of various proceedings in Ukraine and other jurisdictions involving disputes with Ukrainian companies and governmental entities, including litigations with tax authorities, arbitral proceedings and enforcement of arbitral awards.

Related projects:

  • Advising an American investment fund on the first Ukrainian USD 35 million municipal bond default and recovery
  • Advising a British investment company on the first Ukrainian USD 80 million corporate bond default and recovery
  • Advising aggrieved creditors on the bankruptcy of the biggest Ukrainian shipping company for value of claims for more than USD 30 million
  • Advising a leading banking metals business on restructuring of operations and USD 11 million recovery of gold metals from a Ukrainian bank
  • Advising a major American investment fund on the land dispute regarding the double (overlapping) ownership the land plots
  • Successful defence of an US-based investment fund in its dispute over privatized shares with the State Property Fund of Ukraine.
