Energy and Natural Resources sector is considered to be the most prospective in the country. At the same time, due to complex compliance regulations, high risks and numerous peculiarities of every sector, dealing with industry objects requires a deep knowledge of business processes and technology as well as application of complicated transaction schemes. We are well-informed with business practices, processes and technology of this sector and also have the extended experience and deep expertise of using complex transactions, structuring of financial operations, documents processing that are aligned with oil, gas and other natural resources development, debt, sale and investment risk management, resolution of disputes and obtaining of compensations.

We represent clients engaged in every sector of the energy business, including exploration and production companies, natural gas and oil pipelines, storage developers and providers and processors, energy marketers and electrical power generators, drilling and oilfield service contractors, commodity traders and energy finance providers. We also render legal services for electrical and heating energy production and distribution, liquefied gas transportation, and power generation and distribution, including alternative energy sources.

Related projects:

  • Advising a Russian Oil Group on USD 2 billion tax and corporate structuring of operations in Ukraine, retail and tolling arrangements, tax planning and other issues
  • Advising a group of Ukrainian investors on multibillion acquisition and structuring of operations of the biggest Ukrainian heat producer and distributor in Ukraine
  • Advising Ukrainian enterprises and foreign representative offices on optimization of work stuff reduction and massive corporate layoffs
  • Advising the Ukrainian enterprises on the issues of privatization, restructuring and reorganization in the areas of steel, food, oil and gas manufacturing, including a major Russian oil and gas giant and a major Ukrainian food producer.
