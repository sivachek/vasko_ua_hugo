Our attorneys deeply understand agricultural and food industry’s challenges, and have extensive experience in advising companies of this sector. We provide legal advice to agricultural holdings and enterprises and support transactions throughout the production and distribution chain on all aspects of their operations, and also we advise on land use, water and natural resources laws, environmental laws, commercial and trade issues, debt finance, regulatory and other issues.

Our service in food industry is concentrated on companies that produce, process, distribute and sale beverages and food products. Our lawyers provide consultation services on acquisition and sale of titles, state registration, certification, and licensing of food and beverage products, F&B safety certificates, product promotion issues, fulfilment of agreements on supply and distribution; price formation; public procurement, leasing of agricultural lands, etc.

Related projects:

  • Advising a major Ukrainian food producer on restructuring of the activities of five food manufacturing enterprises
  • Advising world’s biggest food producers, pharmaceutical producers and personal hygiene products producer on structuring and commercial operations in Ukraine
  • Advising a major Ukrainian food producer on taxation issues and successful defence in court against the State Tax Administration for the total value of claims of UAH 40 million
  • Advising a major European-based brewing concern in its acquisition of majority stakes in two Ukrainian breweries
  • Advising a major American Investment Fund on USD 25 million agricultural land lease structuring and acquisition in Ukraine.
